# Optical Flow equation 
- $I_{x} u+I_{y} v+\mathrm{I}_{t}=0$ 
	- $I_x$ , $I_y$ , $I_t$ partial derivative of $I$ at $x$ , $y$ and $t$ 
	- $u$ and $v$ : horizontal and vertical speed
